# Barfliz Market Position Brief

**Date:** April 16, 2026
**Prepared by:** Steve Kaplan + Claude (multi-agent analysis)

---

## The Opportunity

No app owns "who's out tonight" + group coordination + chat + virtual gifting in one product. The social nightlife category in the US has no clear winner. Foursquare City Guide shut down Dec 2024. The check-in category is vacant.

**Total addressable market:** US bars and nightclubs $27B. Global $105.3B (2026).

- **a16z** active in IRL social (Partiful at $140M, Plots via SPEEDRUN)
- **Best Nights VC** (Jagermeister fund) is the only nightlife-exclusive VC. $300K-$1M checks. 16 portfolio companies (Plots, 222, XCEED, Thursday).
- **Fever at $527M** consolidating ticketing (DICE acquisition) but not social.

### Market Gaps Barfliz Exploits

| Gap | Evidence | Barfliz Advantage |
|-----|----------|-------------------|
| No winner combines real-time presence + group coordination + chat | Discotech is transactional. Partiful is invitations. Radiate is festival-first. | Swarms + check-ins + chat in one app fills the gap. |
| Virtual gifting untapped in nightlife | No competitor offers virtual drink-sending. TikTok gifting generates $6B+/yr. | Virtual drinks are natural nightlife monetization. |
| 23-country footprint is rare | Most competitors are single-market. | International nightlife travelers need one app. |
| Venue-side monetization via verified foot traffic | Bloc proved pay-per-check-in works (10x ROI, GBP 2/visit). Nobody does this in the US. | Check-ins become a performance ad product. |
| "Who's near me" for nightlife | Snap Map has no venue context. No competitor has trusted social circle + real-time location + nightlife. | Safety + social + nightlife together. |

### Biggest Risks

1. Partiful expanding into nightlife discovery (funding + user base to do it)
2. Radiate v2.0 gaining traction with 3D social map
3. Cold-start problem killing engagement before density is achieved

### The Tinder Playbook (Adapted for Barfliz)

Tinder went from 400 to 4,000 users in one week at USC using three tactics:

1. **Download-as-entry.** Host a party where showing a Barfliz profile is the "cover charge." Tinder's co-founder did this at a USC birthday party.
2. **Campus-by-campus density.** Don't launch "nationally." Launch in Himmarshee + Las Olas (your "campus"). Concentrate density.
3. **Greek life seeding.** Seed the most socially connected groups first. For Barfliz: bar staff, promoters, and regular nightlife crews.

**Barfliz equivalent targets:**
- The Wharf Fort Lauderdale (high foot traffic, casual)
- American Social (waterfront, 25-35 demo)
- Booze Garden (Himmarshee, younger)
- Original Fat Cat's (high volume)
- Rooftop @1WLO (upscale, Instagram-friendly)

### Venue Partnership Program ("Barfliz Night")

1. Pitch 10-15 venues: "We send foot traffic. You give users $1 off their first drink on Thursday."
2. Bar's cost: $0.50-$1.00 per drink (cost of goods on a well shot). Your cost: $0.
3. Table tents, bathroom stickers, coasters with QR codes at partner venues.
4. Volume math: 10 venues x 3 nights/week x 5-10 downloads/venue/night = 150-300 installs/month at $0.

### Campus Ambassador Program

- 3-5 ambassadors at FAU (Boca) and Nova Southeastern (Fort Lauderdale)
- Pay: $50/mo base + $0.25 per verified install via referral code (cap $150/mo)
- Each ambassador hosts one "Barfliz Night" per month
- Volume: 5 ambassadors x 50-150 installs/month = 250-750 installs at $0.15-$0.50 CPI

### Critical Density Threshold

50-100 active users within Las Olas / Himmarshee on a Friday/Saturday night. Below that, the app feels dead. Above that, it feels like discovery.

**How to fake density until achieved:**
1. Show "The Wharf is trending tonight (12 people checked in this week)" even without live users
2. Run concentrated events: one venue, one night, 50 people (better than 50 across 20 bars)
3. Focus on Thu-Sat 8pm-2am only
4. Launch night: coordinate 100+ installs across 3-4 adjacent venues on Himmarshee

### Lush Coin Economy

**Earning (free, seeds behavior):**

| Action | Coins |
|--------|-------|
| First check-in of the night | 5 |
| Weekend streak (3rd week) | 10 |
| Inviting a friend who signs up | 20 |
| Completing profile | 10 |
| Being in a Swarm of 5+ | 10 |
| Daily login | 1-3 (escalating) |

**Spending (the sinks that drive purchases):**

| Item | Coins | Real $ Equivalent |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| Basic virtual drink (beer, wine) | 10-25 | $0.10-$0.25 |
| Premium drink (cocktail, champagne) | 50-200 | $0.50-$2.00 |
| Legendary (bottle service, gold bottle) | 500-2,000 | $5.00-$20.00 |
| Swarm spotlight (pin group on map) | 100 | $1.00 |
| Ghost Mode (1 hour) | 25 | $0.25 |
| Night Planner AI upgrade | 75 | $0.75 |

**Purchase packages (via Apple/Google IAP):**

| Package | Coins | Price | Discount |
|---------|-------|-------|----------|
| Starter | 100 | $0.99 | 0% |
| Popular | 550 | $4.99 | 8% |
| Best Value | 1,200 | $9.99 | 16% |
| Party Pack | 2,800 | $19.99 | 28% |
| Whale Pack | 7,500 | $49.99 | 33% |
| Legend | 16,500 | $99.99 | 39% |

**Economy design principles:**
1. Visible spending (every gift visible to other users at the venue, FOMO drives more spending)
2. Scarcity events (limited-time items tied to real-world events)
3. Leaderboards ("Top Spender at [Venue] tonight")
4. Earn-to-spend ratio: 15-25 free coins/week covers 1-2 basic drinks. Anything above requires purchase.
5. Never let coins expire (Apple requires this for IAP currency)

Revenue math: 5% of venues in a metro (200-500 bars) at $199/mo = 10-25 venues = $2,000-$5,000/mo per city. Scale to 10 cities = $20,000-$50,000/mo B2B alone.

### Critical Risk: Apple IAP Compliance

Apple will reject Solana-based coin purchases that bypass IAP. Route all Lush Coin purchases through Apple/Google IAP (30% cut). Use Solana as the ledger only, not the payment rail. Price packages to absorb the 30%.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Barfliz ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Barfliz mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Summary: We collect location data to show you nearby venues and connect you with friends. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Drink preferences, status updates, and optional bio information.
  • Communications: Messages you send to other users through the App.
  • Payment Information: When using payment features, we process transactions through secure third-party providers (Stripe, Venmo). We do not store your full payment card details.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to show nearby venues and enable check-in features. You can disable location services in your device settings.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent in the App, and crash reports.
  • Geofence Events: Entry and exit events at participating venues when location services are enabled.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Venue Data: We receive venue information from Google Places API to display accurate business details.
  • Music Services: If you connect Spotify or Apple Music, we access your currently playing track (with your permission) to enable music sharing features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and functionality
  • Display nearby venues and show which friends are at specific locations
  • Enable communication between users
  • Process payments and virtual gift transactions
  • Send important notifications about your account and App updates
  • Personalize your experience based on preferences and activity
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Location Data

Important: Location tracking is central to Barfliz's functionality. Here's how we handle it:
  • Venue Check-ins: We use your location to automatically or manually check you into venues.
  • Friend Visibility: Your location at venues may be visible to friends (based on your privacy settings).
  • Ghost Mode: You can enable Ghost Mode to hide your location from all other users.
  • Background Location: If you enable background location, we may track your location when the App is not in active use to provide timely notifications about friends nearby.
  • Data Retention: Location history is retained for 90 days and then automatically deleted.

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

4.1 Other Users

  • Your profile information and venue check-ins (based on your privacy settings)
  • Messages you send directly to other users
  • Music you're currently listening to (if music sharing is enabled)

4.2 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Radar: Geofencing and location services
  • Google: Maps and venue data
  • Stripe/Venmo: Payment processing
  • Firebase: Push notifications and analytics

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable location services, notifications, or marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@barfliz.com or use the in-app settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Location History: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Messages: Retained for 1 year after deletion request
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance

8. Children's Privacy

Barfliz is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction (21+ in the United States, 18+ in Australia). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the legal drinking age. If we discover that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell personal information.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
